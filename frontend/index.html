FAQ

Got Questions?
We've Got Answers.

Everything you need to know about SynkSpace before you join the waitlist.

01 Who can join SynkSpace?
SynkSpace is open to content creators (nano to mega influencers), brands of all sizes, and event organisers across India. Whether you have 1,000 followers or 10 million — if you create authentic content, there's a place for you here.
02 How does the escrow payment system work?
When a brand hires a creator, funds are locked in our secure escrow vault. The money is only released once deliverables are approved — brands don't pay for work not done, and creators are guaranteed payment for completed work.
03 Is SynkSpace free to join?
Joining the waitlist and creating a profile is completely free. We operate on a small success-based commission only when a collaboration is successfully completed. No monthly fees, no hidden charges — we only earn when you do.
04 When will SynkSpace launch publicly?
We're currently in private beta, onboarding our first cohort of creators, brands, and event organisers. Waitlist members get exclusive early access before the public launch — join now to secure your spot and shape the platform from day one.
